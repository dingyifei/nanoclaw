# Best Rate Guarantee (BRG) Quick Reference

**Last updated: 2026-03-24**

| Chain | Claim Window | Pre-Arrival Min | Bonus | Membership Req'd |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|-------|-------------------|
| Hilton | 24h after booking | None | 25% off matched rate | No |
| Marriott | 24h after booking | 24h | 25% off or 5K Bonvoy pts | Yes (Bonvoy) |
| Hyatt | 24h after booking | 24h | 20% off or 5K WoH pts | No |
| IHG | 24h after booking | 24h | 5x IHG pts (cap 40K) | Yes (One Rewards) |
| Accor | 24h after booking | 48h | 25% off (10% for luxury brands) | No |
| Best Western | 24h after booking | — | $100 gift card | No |
| Wyndham | 24h after booking | 48h | 3K Wyndham pts | Yes (Rewards) |
| Choice | 24h after booking | 48h before 6AM | $50 card (US/CA) / free night (intl) | No |

## Key rules (all chains)

- Must be same hotel, room type, dates, guest count, cancellation policy
- Rate must be publicly available (no corporate/opaque/coupon-gated rates)
- Must be ≥1% lower (2% for foreign currency)

## Notable exceptions

- **Marriott**: Tightened verification mid-2025 — they check if rate is still live at review time, not filing time
- **IHG**: Excludes Greater China (Mainland, Macau, HK, Taiwan)
- **Wyndham**: Excludes Asia-Pacific
- **Accor**: Luxury brands (Fairmont, Raffles, Swissôtel) only get 10% off instead of 25%
- **Best Western**: One gift card per person per 30 days; reports of difficulty getting claims honored
