# Gondola.ai — Supported Chains & Rates

**Last updated: 2026-03-24**

## Tier Rates

| Tier | Cashback Rate |
|------|--------------|
| Regular | 3% |
| Silver | 5% |
| Gold | 7% |

## Supported Hotel Chains

Gondola cashback applies **only** to direct bookings with these chains:

- Accor Live Limitless
- Best Western Rewards
- Choice Privileges Rewards
- Hilton Honors
- IHG One Rewards
- Leaders Club
- Marriott Bonvoy
- World of Hyatt
- Wyndham Rewards

**Independent hotels and chains not listed above are NOT supported by Gondola.**

## Usage in This Skill

Gondola.ai is the **preferred browser interface** for checking direct rates at supported chains. It surfaces member rates, AAA rates, and AARP rates from the chain in one place, while automatically earning Gondola Cash on top. Requires login (use persistent browser profile for session persistence). Fall back to the chain's own website if Gondola is unavailable.

## Key Rules

- Gondola Cash = future booking credit on Gondola, valued at face value ($1 = $1)
- **Preserves**: loyalty points at full rate, elite status perks, hotel co-branded CC bonuses
- Charge appears as the hotel on your credit card statement (not Gondola)
- Works with any direct rate variant: member rate, AAA, AARP
- Does NOT stack with cashback portal click-throughs (mutually exclusive)
- Check memory for user's Gondola tier; if not in memory, ask and save
